        <div class="container">
            <h2 class="text-center mb-5 text-primary">Our Application Process</h2>
            <p class="text-center mb-5 lead">Get to know each step in our application process to secure a position at Chandler Aquatic Centre. We value transparency and clarity, guiding you from application submission to joining our team.</p>

            <!-- Process Steps -->
            <div class="row">
                <!-- Step 1 -->
                <div class="col-md-12 mb-5">
                    <h4 class="text-secondary">Step 1: Submit Your Application</h4>
                    <p>Begin by submitting your application form along with a <strong>non-refundable processing fee of $135 USD</strong>. This fee covers the administrative and logistical costs associated with processing your application.</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li><strong>What’s Covered:</strong> Our team will review your application thoroughly, ensuring it meets our standards and criteria for the role.</li>
                        <li><strong>Dedicated Support:</strong> The fee also allows us to provide support throughout the application process, helping answer any questions you may have along the way.</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p class="text-muted">Note: Please ensure your details are accurate and all required documents are attached, as incomplete applications may delay processing.</p>
                </div>

                <!-- Step 2 -->
                <div class="col-md-12 mb-5">
                    <h4 class="text-secondary">Step 2: Application Review (7 Days)</h4>
                    <p>After submitting your application, allow our team up to <strong>7 days</strong> to review your submission. During this period, we evaluate each application based on the role requirements, experience, and other essential criteria.</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li><strong>What We Check:</strong> Our team examines qualifications, experience, and suitability for the role you applied for.</li>
                        <li><strong>Communication:</strong> You will receive an email update on your application status. If accepted, the email will provide instructions for the next steps.</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p class="text-muted">Tip: Ensure your email address is correct and check your inbox and spam folder for updates from us.</p>
                </div>

                <!-- Step 3 -->
                <div class="col-md-12 mb-5">
                    <h4 class="text-secondary">Step 3: Apply for a Work Permit</h4>
                    <p>Once accepted, you’ll proceed to apply for a <strong>work permit</strong>. This step verifies your eligibility to work legally and requires submitting relevant documents along with a <strong>$300 USD processing fee</strong>.</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li><strong>Documents Needed:</strong> Identification, educational certificates, relevant work experience, and any required professional licenses.</li>
                        <li><strong>Why the Fee?</strong> This fee helps cover costs associated with document verification, ensuring your work eligibility and meeting compliance standards.</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p>Work permit approval is crucial as it grants you official permission to work in the country.</p>
                </div>

                <!-- Step 4 -->
                <div class="col-md-12 mb-5">
                    <h4 class="text-secondary">Step 4: Visa Application</h4>
                    <p>After your work permit is approved, apply for a visa to enter and work in Australia. Depending on your role and qualifications, different visa types are available with varying costs:</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li><strong>Skilled Regional Visa:</strong> 360 AUD (~265 USD)</li>
                        <li><strong>Temporary Work Visa:</strong> 310 AUD (~225 USD)</li>
                        <li><strong>Distinguished Talent Visa:</strong> 4,110 AUD (~3,000 USD)</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p>Visa fees are required by Australian immigration authorities. Processing times vary, so we recommend preparing all necessary documents to avoid delays.</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li><strong>Why the Visa Fee?</strong> This fee goes towards processing and evaluating your application, allowing you to work legally in Australia.</li>
                        <li><strong>Assistance Available:</strong> If needed, our team can offer guidance on completing the visa application.</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p class="text-muted">Note: Visa processing can take several weeks depending on your location and visa type.</p>
                </div>

                <!-- Required Documents Summary -->
                <div class="col-md-12 mb-5">
                    <h4 class="text-secondary">Required Documents Summary</h4>
                    <p>To help you prepare, here’s a summary of the documents you may need throughout the application process:</p>
                    <ul>
                        <li><strong>Identification Documents:</strong> Passport, national ID card, or any other valid ID.</li>
                        <li><strong>Educational Certificates:</strong> Degree or diploma relevant to the job position.</li>
                        <li><strong>Work Experience Proof:</strong> Previous employment letters or experience certificates.</li>
                        <li><strong>Professional Licenses (if applicable):</strong> Any licenses or certifications required for your role.</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p>Ensure that you have clear, certified copies of these documents ready to upload as part of your application.</p>
                </div>
